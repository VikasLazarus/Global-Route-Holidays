Dalhousie

Former colonial hill station with temples, palaces and forts built using blended architectural style and stunning landscapes are mesmerizing

“Little Switzerland of India”

Situated on five hills, once the summer retreat place of British Governor-General in India ‘The Earl of Dalhousie’ retains its colonial charisma and attracts thousands of tourists every year. While visiting Dalhousie places that you cannot miss to visit lists long and includes Khajjiar with major attractions like Khajjiar lake, Kalatop Khajjiar Sanctuary, Lord Shiva Statue, Golden Devi temple. Satdhara Fall and Panchpula for Photography, waterfall, picnic, monuments and sightseeing, Click some amazing pictures at Chamera Lake, Rang Mahal, Daikund Peak, Kalatop Wildlife Reserve.
