Day 3
Full day Solang Valley sightseeing by cab

Today morning leave the hotel and start traveling towards Solang Valley. Solang Valley is the most visited destination in the Manali region, located at the distance of Approximately 14 KM from the main town). This place serves with a wide range variety of activities and adventure sports. Here you can enjoy paragliding, Zorbing, skiing, at a very nominal cost. There are overhead cable car rides available as well and you can also enjoy horse riding too. Also visit Anjani Mahadev Temple there. After that return back to the hotel at Manali. On the way, Vashisht Temple & village, Take a bath in hot water spring there. Evening time visit the famous Mall Road and after doing some shopping returns back to the hotel. Overnight stay in a hotel.

Day 4
Manali to Delhi drop (Day journey by cab)

Today Morning check-out from a hotel and drive towards Delhi. Day journey by cab from Manali to Delhi. On the way visit Naggar Castle. Naggar is one the most visited destination in Kullu District. It is an ancient heritage village (popular for the Naggar Castle). Naggar Castle is a unique example of the Western and Himalayan style of architecture. The entire campus is made up of Wood and stones. It also provides a 360° view of the surrounding areas. Also, visit Tripura Sundari Temple, Roerich art gallery & Museum, Uruswati Himalayan Folk Art Museum, and Gauri Shankar Temple. After that drive towards Delhi. Reach in Delhi by evening time and drop at Delhi Railway Station /Airport.
